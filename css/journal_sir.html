International Journal of Entomological Research

International Journal of Entomological Research (IJER) is an open access, international, periodically published journal of eSci Journals Publishing. Journal publishes original as well as extended versions of published research including theoretical contributions, conferences, critical reviews, commentaries, term papers and short communications on the evolution, biology, morphology, physiology, anatomy, taxonomy, ecology, development, behavior, toxicology, genetics and systematics of both recent and fossil insects. Journal covers the whole field of general, experimental, systemic and applied entomology and acarology. We mainly focus to process submissions promptly, and to screen them strictly but fairly in order to maintain the quality of journal.
